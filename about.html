Our Story

About Nexoria

Born from a love of survival gameplay and a frustration with overcrowded, pay-to-win servers.

Built for the long run

Nexoria started with a simple idea: build a server we'd actually want to play on. No exploitative monetisation, no overpowered donor kits, no shortcuts. Just a well-maintained, feature-rich survival experience backed by a team that actually cares.

Running on premium EU infrastructure, Nexoria delivers consistent performance for players across Europe and beyond. We obsess over TPS stability, anti-cheat accuracy and plugin quality so you never have to think about the technical side — just play.

Our economy, shop, and job systems are carefully tuned to keep the market healthy and rewarding for both casual players and dedicated grinders alike.
