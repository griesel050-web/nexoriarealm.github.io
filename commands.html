General Essentials · GSit · InvSeePlusPlus · SkinsRestorer
/help
All
Open the in-game help menu with a paginated command list.
/spawn
All
Teleport back to the main server spawn point.
/rules
All
Display the server rules in chat.
/discord
All
Get the Discord invite link posted in chat.
/afk
All
Toggle AFK status manually. AxAFKZone handles auto-AFK after inactivity.
/sit
All
Sit on the ground using GSit. Right-click stairs or slabs to sit on them naturally.
/report <player> <reason>
All
Report a rule-breaking player directly to the moderation team.
/invsee <player>
All
View another player's inventory via InvSeePlusPlus (limited to staff for ender chest).
/skin <username>
VIP+
Change your displayed skin to any Minecraft account's skin via SkinsRestorer.
/skin reset
VIP+
Reset your skin back to your original Minecraft account skin.

/ah selling

Claims GriefPrevention · WorldGuard
/claim
All
Claim the chunk you're standing in. Use a golden shovel to create custom-shaped claims.
/unclaim
All
Remove the claim you are currently standing inside.
/claimlist
All
View all your active claims and your remaining claim block balance.
/trust <player>
All
Grant a player full build and interact access inside your claim.
/containertrust <player>
All
Allow a player to open chests and containers in your claim without build access.
/accesstrust <player>
All
Allow a player to use buttons, levers and pressure plates in your claim only.
/untrust <player>
All
Remove a player's access from your claim entirely.
/trustlist
All
View all players trusted in the claim you are currently standing in.
/abandonclaim
All
Delete the claim you're standing in and refund all its blocks back to your balance.
Homes & Warps Essentials · AxPlayerWarps
/sethome [name]
All
Save your location as a home. Limits: Member=1, VIP=3, Elite=6, God=12, MVP=unlimited.
/home [name]
All
Teleport to a saved home. Run /home without a name to see your list.
/homes
All
List all your saved home locations and their coordinates.
/delhome <name>
All
Delete a saved home by name.
/warp [name]
All
Teleport to a server warp. Run /warp alone to list all available warps.
/pwarp [name]
All
Browse or travel to a player-created public warp via AxPlayerWarps.
/pwarp set <name>
VIP+
Create a public player warp at your current location for others to use.
/pwarp delete <name>
VIP+
Delete one of your existing player warps.
