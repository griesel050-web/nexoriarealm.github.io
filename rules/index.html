Server Rules

Breaking rules results in mutes, bans or permanent removal. Ignorance is not an excuse — read them.

1General
1.1
Respect everyone. Treat all players and staff with basic respect. Harassment, bullying or personal attacks of any kind will not be tolerated.
Temp Ban
1.2
English in global chat. Use English in global chat so staff can moderate effectively. Other languages are fine in private messages or team chat.
Warn
1.3
No advertising. Do not advertise other Minecraft servers, Discord servers or websites in chat, DMs or signs.
Perm Ban
1.4
One account per player. Alt accounts are not permitted. Using alts to bypass punishments results in permanent bans on all accounts.
Perm Ban
1.5
Follow staff instructions. If a staff member asks you to stop doing something, stop. Arguing or refusing is a punishable offence.
Temp Ban
2Chat
2.1
No toxicity or hate speech. Racism, sexism, homophobia or any discriminatory language — zero tolerance, immediate punishment.
Perm Ban
2.2
No excessive caps or spam. Don't spam chat with repeated messages or excessive capital letters. It disrupts the community.
Mute
2.3
No NSFW content. Keep chat clean and appropriate for all ages. No sexual, graphic or disturbing content.
Temp Ban
2.4
No threatening behaviour. Real-life threats, doxxing or sharing personal information about any player is an immediate permanent ban.
Perm Ban
2.5
No misleading trade chat. Don't advertise false prices or scam other players in trade. All scams are bannable.
Temp Ban
3Gameplay
3.1
No hacking or cheating. Any client modification that gives an unfair advantage — X-ray, fly hacks, kill-aura, auto-clicker — is a permanent ban. Vulcan detects most automatically.
Perm Ban
3.2
No griefing. Destroying, stealing from or modifying another player's builds or claimed land is strictly prohibited. Use GriefPrevention to protect your area.
Temp Ban
3.3
No exploiting bugs. If you discover a bug or exploit, report it to staff immediately. Abusing exploits for personal gain is a bannable offence.
Temp Ban
3.4
No AFK farming machines. Auto-clickers, AFK machines or methods designed to farm resources while unattended are not allowed. AxAFKZone will move you automatically.
Warn → Ban
3.5
No trap claims. Setting up claims designed to trap or kill other players is prohibited.
Temp Ban
4Building
4.1
No inappropriate builds. Buildings that are offensive, sexual or represent hate symbols will be removed without warning.
Temp Ban
4.2
No lag machines. Do not build contraptions designed to cause server lag. This includes massive redstone machines, mob spawner farms beyond reasonable limits and entity-spam builds.
Temp Ban
4.3
Claim your builds. Always protect your builds with GriefPrevention. Staff cannot recover unprotected builds that are griefed.
Advisory
5Economy
5.1
No scamming. All agreed trades must be honoured. Scamming players out of items, money or services is a bannable offence.
Temp Ban
5.2
No real-money trading. Selling in-game items, currency or accounts for real money outside the official store is prohibited.
Perm Ban
5.3
No market manipulation. Artificially inflating or crashing item prices to harm the economy is not allowed.
Warn → Ban
6Staff
6.1
Do not impersonate staff. Pretending to be a staff member or claiming to have staff powers you don't have is an immediate ban.
Perm Ban
6.2
Staff decisions are final. If you disagree with a punishment, appeal it via our contact form or Discord. Do not argue in public chat.
Mute
6.3
Report rule-breaking. Use /report <player> <reason> or open a ticket in Discord. Don't take matters into your own hands.
Advisory
7Punishments
⚠️
Warning

First offence for minor rules. Recorded and escalates on repeat.

🔇
Mute

Restricted from chatting for a set duration. Gameplay continues.

🚫
Temp Ban

Removed from the server for hours to weeks. Account intact on return.

🔨
Perm Ban

Permanent removal. Appeal via Discord — not guaranteed acceptance.

Ban Appeals: Submit a ban appeal via our contact form or open a ticket in Discord. Include your IGN, reason for ban and why you should be unbanned. Staff review all appeals within 48 hours.
