Getting Started
The server IP is play.nexoriarealm.xyz. You can click the IP anywhere on this website to copy it to your clipboard automatically.
Nexoria runs on Java Edition. We support the latest version as well as older versions thanks to ViaVersion and ViaBackwards. Bedrock Edition is not supported.
Yes — completely free. You can join, play and enjoy the full survival experience at no cost. The store sells optional cosmetic and quality-of-life upgrades to support the server, but nothing you can't achieve through gameplay.
Nexoria is hosted on premium EU infrastructure, giving European players the best possible ping. Players from other regions can still join — performance will vary by location.
Join with play.nexoriarealm.xyz, read the rules with /rules, then use /rtp to land in the wilderness and start building. Use /sethome to save your base location, and /shop to buy starter resources.
Gameplay
Use GriefPrevention — equip a golden shovel and right-click two corners to claim an area. You start with a free claim block allowance. Use /claimlist to see your claims and /trust <player> to let friends build in your area.
Several ways: sell items at /shop, list items on the /ah auction house, complete AuraSkills jobs, vote with /vote for reward bonuses, or gamble with /coinflip. The richest players usually combine all of these.
Allowed: Optifine, Sodium, Fabric/Forge performance mods, minimap mods (no entity radar), and any purely visual texture pack. Not allowed: X-ray, kill-aura, auto-clicker, fly mods, schematica printer or any mod that automates gameplay. When in doubt, ask in Discord.
AuraSkills is an RPG skill system. As you mine, farm, fight and craft, you level up 15+ skills that permanently boost your stats — more health, stronger attacks, better luck. Open the skill menu anytime with /skills.
Use /ah to open the auction house. To sell: hold the item and type /ah sell <price>. Listings stay live for 48 hours — collect unsold items with /ah expired. Listing limits depend on your rank (Member = 5, MVP = 18).
Use /team create <name> to start a team and /team invite <player> to recruit. Friends can join with /team join <name>. Teams share a private chat channel via /team chat.
Ranks & Store
There are 4 donor ranks: VIP ($4.99), Elite ($9.99), God ($14.99) and MVP ($19.99). Each is a one-time purchase. Visit the store or ranks page to see the full perk breakdown.
No. Donor ranks unlock quality-of-life features like extra homes, flight in your claims and cosmetics — but nothing that makes you more powerful in combat or gives you items free players can't also earn. Skill and grind always wins here.
Ranks are delivered instantly via Tebex once payment is confirmed. If your rank hasn't appeared within 10 minutes, open a ticket in our Discord with your order ID and IGN.
Yes — rank upgrades are available in the store. You only pay the difference between your current rank and the new one. Contact us via the contact page or Discord if you need help with an upgrade.
Technical
Submit a ban appeal via our contact form or open a ticket in Discord. Include your IGN and why you believe the ban is incorrect. We review all appeals within 48 hours.
Report the bug immediately in Discord with screenshots or video evidence. Item restoration is handled case-by-case. We cannot restore items lost to normal gameplay (e.g. dying in lava) — only verified bugs.
Join our Discord server then type /discord link in-game. Follow the instructions that appear in chat. Linking enables cross-platform chat and Discord-exclusive perks.
Type /vote in-game to get a list of voting sites. Vote on each daily. Rewards include coins, PlayerPoints and crate keys. Build a streak for bonus rewards, and contribute to the vote party that rewards everyone when the server hits its daily target.
